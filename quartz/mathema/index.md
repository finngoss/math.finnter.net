'Mathema' is the Greek word meaning "something learned" and the root of our word 'mathematics!' I'm sure to encounter many mathemata on my journey towards becoming a mathematician, so I'm beginning this blog as my repository to keep track of things such as class notes, takeaways from papers I've read, notions about various fields of math, research questions, or anything else I may find interesting.
