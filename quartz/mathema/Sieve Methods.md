[[Analytic Number Theory]]

Sieve methods rely on the basic mathematical principle of inclusion and exclusion.

The quintessential example is the **Sieve of Eratosthenes**, an algorithm for calculating prime numbers.




The Euler product of the Riemann Zeta function is effectively a multiplicative sieve.
