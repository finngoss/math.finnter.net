## Transition to graduate school
- integration workshop: right at the beginning of august. work with other people in your cohort. learn how to work and talk about mathematics. main thing is getting to know peopel and learning how to work with people on mathematics
- TA training. first year assisting an instructor.
- student success class fall semester. meets once a week. read literature on graduate school. talk about UA program.
- community connversation. new to the integration workshop and student succcess. get together a few times a semester to check in and talk about challenges/differences.

# how is the program structured?
phases of the PhD program
- Core Phase: master a common core of fundamental mathematics, qualifying exams <3
- specialization phase: advanced coursework, research experience, indepenetned study, oral exam (can you read literature?). Reach the boundary of current mathematics...
- Disseratation phase: push the boundary further out!

# funding
- types of funding
	- TA: variety of teaching, co-teaching, assistant. 
	- first year: you will be assisting in the class of an instructor. be in the class, helping facilitate active learning, group work, but also helping with grading. office hours. lectures twice during the semester.
	- Graduate fellowship. separate letter. sometimes supplements a half TA.
	- expect to be funded minimally, health insurance, and full tuition.
	- graduate access fellowships. first year students
	- Additional funding sources. RA, outreach scholar, universitty felow, robin, likins, harvill, grogan
- Amount of funding: $25,654 for 9 months
	- in other 3 months, you can do something else. a course you can teach over the summer. or do an internship.
	- cost of rent: $700-900/month for shared.
- Total of 5-6 years with satisfactory progress!
