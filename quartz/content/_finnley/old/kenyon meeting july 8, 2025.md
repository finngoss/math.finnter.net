# My idea for a thesis!
Lattice Point visibility, on any type of integral lattice!

For any additive subgroup $\Lambda\subset\Z^n$, what is the density of points visible from x for:
- $x\in\Lambda$
- $x\in\Z^n$

These are the spans of $m\times n$ matrices! I want to basically study the set of points $\{x\in\Z^n : gcd(Mx)=1\}$

- what machinery will I use? 
	- Analytic number theory...
	- Linear Algebra
	- SageMath
